Our Story

I don’t know exactly where this road is taking us… but I know I’m grateful I’m walking it with you. Every day with you feels like a new chapter sometimes calm, sometimes unpredictable, sometimes full of laughter, sometimes quiet and soft yet every moment somehow becomes beautiful because you are in it. You didn’t just come into my life, you gently changed it. You made ordinary days feel special and simple conversations feel like memories I never want to forget. This journey we are on isn’t perfect, but it’s real. We are learning each other’s fears, strengths, moods, and dreams… and I love that we are growing, not apart, but together. I love how you understand me even when I don’t say much, how your presence alone gives me peace, and how my heart feels safe with you. No matter how long the road becomes, I don’t want to rush it. I want to experience every step — the late-night talks, the teasing, the disagreements we fix, the laughter that hurts our stomachs, and the quiet moments where words are not needed. I’m not just here for the happy days. I’m here for the confusing days, the stressful days, the tired days… I’m here for you. Because loving you is not just a feeling to me, it has become a decision I make every single day. So wherever this journey leads, just know one thing ’m not holding your hand for a moment, I’m holding it for the story we are writing together. And honestly… I wouldn’t want to travel this life with anyone else but you.

Colossians 3:14
“And over all these virtues put on love, which binds them all together in perfect unity.”
